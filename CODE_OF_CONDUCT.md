# Code of Conduct

This project follows a respectful, inclusive collaboration standard.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical issues, not personal attacks.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating.
- Do not share private or sensitive information publicly.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or intimidation
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith engagement

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, lock threads, or restrict participation for violations.

For serious incidents, open a private security advisory report as described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
